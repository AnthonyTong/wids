Get ready for Hong kong's First World Internet Developer Submit! Hosted by Internet Society Hong Kong, Cyberport and Hong Kong Science Parks, the action packed two days summit is the first and may be the biggest cross platform developer summit in the region.

Get connected with the greatest minds in the industry with the latest Internet development, news, various development platform techniques, hints and tips.  We have invited speakers from Microsoft, Google, Facebook, Yahoo!, Mozilla and more who are coming all together with motivating and inspirational talks and workshops.  Hundreds of developers will gather at to meet, learn and exchange experiences and ideas on different development platform/ environment.

The main theme of the event is HTML5 and it will be divided into two parts: conference and workshops.   The workshops will provide a two way communication platform for developers to obtain hands-on experience in small groups to learn new technique, share experience and exchange ideas.
